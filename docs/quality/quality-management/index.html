    Quality management

    Kili Technology is designed to facilitate quality control, while scaling up.

    This is done through

    • Instructions, which allows to illustrate the annotation task on concrete examples. These instructions are made available on the annotation interface, so that annotators can easily refer to it.

    • Consensus, which allows you to have all or part of the dataset annotated several times, in order to measure a consensus score between the annotators.

    • Honeypot, or Golden Standard, which helps to set a ground truth, to audit the quality of the production of annotators throughout the annotation process.

    These indicators are calculated to allow you to to monitor the quality of production, and to act very quickly and iterate with annotators, to help them to produce a better data.
