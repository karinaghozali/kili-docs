    How to load text and image data from a workstation

    The data can be uploaded from the Dataset tab, by clicking on ADD NEW.

    Your data is then securely stored in a dedicated bucket on our cloud. Only you have access to it. You are the owner of the stored data.

    Load the data

    Text & image

    Select the data on your computer to download it.
