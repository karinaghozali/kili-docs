    Simple and intuitive interfaces

    Interfaces are the graphical interfaces on which are performed the annotation or labelling tasks.

    Kili Technology offers a catalogue of simple and intuitive interfaces, configurable to cover the main cases of machine use learning.

    • Image

    • NER & relationship extraction

    • Image transcription / OCR

    • Classification

    • Feeling

    • Voice to text / Speech to text

    The configuration of the interfaces allows them to be adapted to your different cases of use, such as the creation of specific classes.

    Interfaces allow quick access to annotation instructions created at the beginning of the project.
