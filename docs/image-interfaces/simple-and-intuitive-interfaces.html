    Simple and intuitive interfaces

    Interfaces are the graphical interfaces on which are performed the annotation or labelling tasks.

    Kili Technology offers a catalogue of simple and intuitive interfaces, configurable to cover the main cases of machine learning.

    • Image (object recognition, segmentation, classification, transcription)

    • Text (named entities recognition, relations extraction, classification, transcription)

    • Video (transcription, classification)

    • Audio (transcription, classification)

    Theses interfaces can be highly customised to adress all use cases. See how in customize interface section.
